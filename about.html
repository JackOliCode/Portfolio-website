This page is all about me, what I enjoy doing and where I'm wanting to go

I’m a creative and competitive professional, with a Bachelors degree majoring in English Literature and Classical studies, who focuses not just on the outcome but how the journey will affect both the customer and our business and I am lucky to have worked for internationally recognised companies and within the publishing industry, an interesting, ever changing experience.

My previous work experience has required me to be confident working under pressure while both leading or following and I am able to self manage, de-escalate difficult situations and remain flexible to possible changes.

My number one passion in life is traveling and this was the reason for my move to London in 2018. I decided I wanted to work in web development while traveling through Greece and finding myself frustrated trying to navigate websites for restaurants and accommodation. I thought ‘someone really should make a website for these businesses’ and decided that someone could be me.

Since then, I have completed a Full Stack Development program with CareerFoundry and I am looking for Front-End developer roles in London. You can check out my recent projects here and feel free to download my CV below and reach out via my contact links.
